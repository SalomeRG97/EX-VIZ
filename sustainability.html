Sustainability

At EX-VIZ SOLAR, we understand sustainability as the resulting effect of bringing new technologies closer through digital solutions. Developed by ethical human capital and innovative intellectual capital, these solutions empower individuals, businesses, and governments with access to specialized services that ensure greater transparency, cost reduction, and the mitigation of environmental and socioeconomic impacts.

Contribution to the Sustainable Development Goals

Good Health and Well-being

  • We preserve people's lives.
  • We minimize exposure to project risks.
  • We collaborate in the design of preventive safety measures.

Responsible Consumption and Production

  • We contribute to the sustainable management and efficient use of natural resources.
  • We generate valuable data to present in corporate sustainability reports.

Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

  • We turn reality into digital assets that are reliable, sustainable, resilient, and of high quality.
  • We make our solutions accessible to any user with internet and a smartphone, tablet, or laptop.
  • We enhance the technological capabilities of multiple industrial sectors in developing countries.

Climate Action

  • We strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related hazards and natural disasters.
  • We improve education, awareness, and human capacity regarding climate change mitigation.

Sustainable Cities and Communities

  • We build 3D environments that drive inclusive and sustainable urbanization.
  • We optimize territorial management with risk and disaster management inputs.
  • We participate in the pre-feasibility of new public services and infrastructure projects.

Life on Land

  • We ensure the conservation of mountain ecosystems, including their biodiversity.
  • We integrate ecosystem and biodiversity values into national and local planning, development processes, poverty reduction strategies, and accounting.

Partnerships for the Goals

  • We use technology to improve safety and the creation of more sustainable projects through engineering solutions.
  • We streamline the processes of multiple industries by capturing reality in a low-risk virtual environment.
  • We enable high-precision testing and analysis based on digitized information.
