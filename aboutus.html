About Us

Our History

EX-VIEW SOLAR:
The strategic alliance of four market leaders

EBEDIX, a pioneer in AI and digital engineering, Drones Sky Solutions, a leader in solar data capture with drones, and FlyNorth, an expert in thermographic analysis and certification, have united to transform the management of solar parks worldwide.

This alliance gives rise to EX-VIEW Solar, a SaaS solution designed to accelerate the adoption of world-class technologies at affordable prices. In a context where traditional solutions are often expensive, charge per megawatt, or limit analysis with fragmented tools, EX-VIEW Solar emerges as a comprehensive, efficient alternative customized for the real needs of the energy sector.

EX-View Solar integrates data capture, intelligent analysis, and operational action into a single platform, with an all-inclusive monthly subscription model, zero hidden costs, and no additional charges for essential functions.

With headquarters in Medellin, Miami, and Madrid, EX-VIEW Solar provides local and international operational capabilities and software for all our clients.
