4K Touch Wall Software

Transform your sales gallery with massive, interactive 4K touch screens. Provide your agents with the ultimate presentation tool, allowing them to showcase floor plans, views, and amenities with a flick of a finger.

Key Features

  • Ultra-High Resolution: Stunning 4K visuals that render architectural details immaculately.
  • Fluid Interactivity: Multi-touch capabilities allowing zoom, pan, and real-time navigation.
  • Inventory Syncing: Live updates showing which units are available directly on the presentation screen.

The Result

A jaw-dropping focal point in your sales center that transforms casual walk-ins into highly engaged, confident buyers.

Cutting-Edge Immersive Experiences

Offer an unparalleled sense of presence and scale before construction even begins. Virtual Reality transports your clients directly into the property, allowing them to feel the space and appreciate premium finishes.

Key Features

  • Hyper-Real Immersion: 1:1 scale representation of properties.
  • Guided VR Tours: Agents can monitor and guide what the client sees in VR on an external screen.
  • Finish Customization: Clients can swap materials in real-time while fully immersed.

The Result

Eliminates buyer hesitation by providing an emotionally impactful and realistic spatial experience.

Physical Scale Models

We bridge the physical and digital. By overlaying Augmented Reality (AR) or projection mapping onto beautifully crafted physical architectural models, we bring traditional models to life.

Key Features

  • Projection Mapping: Illuminate specific units, amenities, or lighting conditions directly onto a physical model.
  • AR Integration: Clients use tablets to scan the physical layout and see hidden data or interiors.
  • Tactile Attraction: The physical gravitas of a scale model, enhanced with dynamic digital data.

The Result

A stunning, hybrid visualization centerpiece that merges traditional luxury with futuristic technology.
