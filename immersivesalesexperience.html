Physical Scale Models

Your physical scale models are enhanced with interactive technologies that transform them into dynamic storytelling platforms. Through augmented reality overlays and interactive surfaces, static models become living environments — revealing layers of information, availability, and design variations in real time. By combining physical craftsmanship with digital intelligence, we create hybrid experiences that bridge the gap between imagination and reality — delivering a powerful “wow” moment from the very first interaction.

Key Features

  • Augmented Reality Overlays: Buyers can point a tablet or screen at the model to instantly visualize unit details, interior layouts, pricing, and availability — directly on top of the physical environment.
  • Interactive Tables & Touch Displays: Touch-enabled surfaces transform the model into a control interface, allowing users to explore project phases, highlight amenities, and filter units dynamically.
  • Dynamic Project Exploration: Users can navigate the project intuitively — from macro views of the development down to specific units — without needing technical explanations.
  • At-Home Luxury Immersion: Selected buyers receive a curated, high-end package at home — featuring an Apple Vision Pro with a fully customized immersive journey. Instantly, the property comes to life around them. No travel. No showroom. Just a deep personal experience designed to inspire confident decisions.

The Result

A tactile and immersive experience that captures attention, simplifies complexity, and accelerates decision-making. Beyond the visual impact, this approach transforms the traditional showroom into an interactive sales environment — reducing friction, enhancing engagement, and ultimately driving faster conversions.
