High-Fidelity 3D Model

A high-fidelity virtual replica of your architectural design, property, or development—created to transform how projects are showcased, sold, and experienced.

Key Features

  • Cost Efficiency: Eliminate expensive sample models or show houses, reducing construction and logistics costs.
  • Speed to Market: Showcase projects instantly and generate revenue faster, without waiting months for physical models.
  • Global Accessibility: Provide clients with a full site visit from anywhere in the world, removing geographic barriers and expanding reach.
  • Immersive Experience: Deliver realistic 3D detail that recreates the emotional impact of being physically present, building confidence and driving decisions.

The Result

A smarter, faster, and more cost-effective way to present real estate—empowering sales teams, inspiring client trust, and accelerating revenue growth.

Unit Configurator

The Unit Configurator is an interactive 3D platform that connects your high-fidelity digital model with real-time data and transforms your property showcase into an intelligent sales tool. Clients can explore units, check availability, configure finishes, and visualize their future property from anywhere in the world. By integrating directly with your internal systems such as inventory, materials, specifications, and floor plans, the platform creates a transparent, immersive, and personalized buying experience while streamlining the sales process for your team.

Key Features

  • Interactive 3D Model: Explore the entire project through a high-fidelity digital twin.
  • Real-Time Availability: Connects with your sales system to display available, reserved, or sold units.
  • Material & Finish Configurator: Buyers can visualize different materials, finishes, and design options.
  • Integrated Floor Plans: Access detailed layouts and spatial information for each unit.
  • Inventory Integration: Syncs with internal databases to manage materials and configurations.
  • Remote Sales Experience: Clients can explore and configure units from anywhere in the world.

The Result

A faster, smarter, and more transparent real estate sales process that expands your market reach, increases buyer confidence, and empowers your sales team to close deals more efficiently.

Dynamic Pricing & Smart Quoting

This solution transforms property data into actionable sales output. It automatically generates tailored proposals based on customer preferences, unit specifications, and available inventory—delivering accuracy, transparency, and speed. Designed to operate independently, it connects seamlessly with your CRM, digital channels, and sales platforms, ensuring proposals are ready to share across every touchpoint.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Pricing Engine: Instantly calculates costs as clients select layouts, finishes, and upgrades.
  • CRM Integration: Syncs with customer records to personalize proposals and track engagement.
  • Digital Channel Connectivity: Distributes proposals through email, portals, or sales apps.
  • Sales System Integration: Aligns with Unit Configurator and inventory, availability, and materials for real-time accuracy.
  • Automated Proposal Generation: Delivers professional, ready-to-share documents instantly.
  • Transparency & Trust: Shows clients exactly how choices affect pricing, building confidence.

The Result

A faster, smarter, and more connected sales process—empowering teams to close deals quickly, enabling buyers to make confident decisions, and unlocking new revenue opportunities through customization and upselling.

Web Virtual Showroom

The Web Virtual Showroom delivers seamless and cost-efficient streaming through our platform, allowing multiple clients worldwide to interact with your Digital Twin simultaneously—without per-minute streaming fees. This significantly reduces operational costs while providing a smooth and scalable experience. Beyond visualization, the showroom connects your Digital Twin with real-time sales tools. Buyers can explore properties, review units, and complete transactions from anywhere. Integrated with CRM systems, digital channels, secure payment platforms, and e-signature solutions like DocuSign, it enables a complete end-to-end digital buying journey.

Key Features

  • Seamless Streaming Technology: Hosted on our platform, eliminating expensive per-minute streaming fees.
  • Immersive Digital Twin Interaction: Explore properties and units from anywhere with unlimited concurrent users.
  • Real-Time Availability: Display available, reserved, or sold units directly from your sales system.
  • Customization Options: Configure finishes, layouts, and upgrades instantly.
  • Automated Documentation: Generate proposals and contracts with integrated e-signature workflows.
  • Secure Online Payments: Reserve units with fast and secure digital transactions.
  • CRM & Channel Integration: Connect customer data, portals, and sales platforms in one experience.

The Result

A fully digital showroom that expands global reach, accelerates sales, reduces reliance on physical showrooms, and builds buyer confidence through transparency and convenience.

Analytics Dashboard

The Analytics Dashboard converts user interactions into strategic insights for developers, investors, and project managers. By analyzing how users explore the digital model, interact with units, and engage with the project, it provides a clear view of market interest, buyer behavior, and project performance. Beyond simple metrics, the dashboard becomes a dynamic reporting mechanism that helps stakeholders evaluate demand, optimize marketing strategies, and monitor real-time engagement with the project. These insights support smarter decisions in campaign strategy, product positioning, and investment performance.

Key Features

  • User Interaction Tracking: Monitor how visitors explore the digital project and interact with units and features.
  • Demand & Buyer Insights: Identify which units, amenities, or configurations generate the most interest.
  • Engagement Analytics: Measure user behavior, time spent, and exploration patterns across the experience.
  • Campaign Performance Tracking: Evaluate how marketing channels and campaigns drive engagement and demand.
  • Dynamic Project Reporting: Generate clear reports that help investors and stakeholders track project performance.
  • Visual Data Dashboard: Present key metrics through intuitive charts and real-time data visualization.

The Result

A powerful intelligence tool that helps investors, developers, and sales teams understand demand, measure campaign performance, and make informed strategic decisions based on real user engagement.

Floor-Level View Simulation

Recreate the exact real-world view from every floor of a future building by combining drone imagery with precise 3D positioning. This allows developers and buyers to understand visibility, surroundings, and real or simulate skyline views.

Key Features

  • Drone Imagery Integration: High-resolution aerial photography captured directly from the project site.
  • Exact Floor Perspectives: Simulate the real view from every level of the building with accurate positioning.
  • Real Environment Context: Visualize surrounding buildings, landscapes, and skyline conditions.
  • Pre-construction Visualization: Understand views and sightlines before the building is constructed.

The Result

A realistic preview of what residents or visitors will actually see from diferent prospectives, helping developers present projects with greater confidence and transparency.
